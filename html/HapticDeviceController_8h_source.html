HapticDeviceController.h
1/*
2 * HapticDeviceController.h
3 *
4 * This controller implements a bilateral haptic teleoperation scheme in
5 * open loop. The commands are computed for the haptic device (force feedback)
6 * and the controlled robot (desired task). HapticDeviceController includes
7 * impedance-type and admittance-type controllers, with plane/line/orientation
8 * guidances, and the workspace mapping algorithm.
9 *
10 * Authors: Margot Vulliez & Mikael Jorda
11 */
12
