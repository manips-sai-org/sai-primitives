2 * SingularityHandling.h
3 *
4 * This class creates a singularity classifying and handling class for
5 * type 1 and type 2 singularities. The singularity strategy linearly blends
6 * the torques from the singular task directions and the torques from the
7 * singularity torque strategy, and adds this to the torques from the
8 * non-singular task directions.

10 * Author: William Chong
